## Overview

This article explains key ideas in **SOC & Detection** with a focus on awareness, governance, and safe technical learning.

## Key Points

- Understand the risk before choosing tools.
- Prefer defensive validation over assumptions.
- Document findings clearly and ethically.

## Practical Takeaway

Security maturity improves when teams combine education, policy, monitoring, and continuous QA.
